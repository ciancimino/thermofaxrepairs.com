About Us

Thermofax Repair Seattle, founded by the seasoned tattoo artist Bismark Pinera of Electric Cobra Tattoo, stands as the culmination of over 20 years of rich experience in the art of tattooing, coupled with a decade-long expertise in Thermofax repairing. Born out of a passion for providing top-notch Thermofax repair services, Thermofax Repair Seattle was established to address the demand for excellence in Thermofax repairing and reselling machines. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry, making us the go-to destination for all your Thermofax repair needs.
