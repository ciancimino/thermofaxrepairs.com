FAQ's

1. What is Thermofax Repair Seattle?

Thermofax Repair Seattle is a service founded by tattoo artist Bismark Pinera, specializing in Thermofax machine repair and resale. With over 10 years of repair experience, we provide top-notch thermofax repair services.

2. How long does Thermofax repair take?

Repairs typically span from 3 days to 3 weeks, contingent on the selected services. For emergencies requiring expedited repair service, Contact Us directly; additional charges may apply.

3. What are the costs for Thermofax Repair services?

Our services commence at $425, with pricing scaling based on the specific Thermofax Repair services required.

4. Do you purchase Thermofax machines?

Absolutely, we buy Thermofax machines in most conditions. Contact Us and share pictures of your Thermofax machine along with your asking price.

5. Do you sell Thermofax machines or parts?

Certainly, we offer Thermofax machines and parts for sale. For pricing and availability, or to stay updated, follow us on Instagram. We announce new machines or parts as they become available for stencil repair. Our Store will be launching soon.

6. Is there a warranty for Thermofax repair or part orders?

We provide a 2-month warranty against defective parts. If you encounter any issues with your tattoo stencil machine, Contact Us promptly for troubleshooting.

7. Do you offer a warranty on the machines you sell?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive 4-month warranty on Thermofax machines we sell. This warranty covers repairs, parts, and troubleshooting for tattoo stencil printers, though refunds are not available. Contact Us if you need assistance.
